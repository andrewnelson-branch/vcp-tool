Executive Summary

Mission: Establish complete control over every claimed vehicle within 24-48 hours of FNOL to minimize storage charges, reduce economic total losses, and ensure optimal claim outcomes.

Three-Pronged Strategy

1. Control Every Vehicle Fast

Manual daily task creation ensures no vehicle sits in limbo. Within 48 hours, every claim is at GYB or Copart.

2. Decide Total Loss Early

Manual early TL task creation activates specialist workflow immediately, cutting settlement time to 12 days.

3. Zero Tolerance for Waste

GYB delivers certified repairs with cost controls. Customer-selected shops get enhanced oversight to prevent overruns.

Key Success Metrics

Metric Target
Vehicle Control Rate (within 48hrs) 95%
Daily Location Task Completion (by 10am) 100%
Total Loss Cycle Time 12 days
Average Advance Charges <$1,500
GYB Utilization 45% of all claims

Expected Outcomes

  • Reduced storage costs through rapid routing and $0 storage during coverage resolution
  • Lower total loss ratio via early identification and parallel processing
  • Faster claim closure (12-day TL cycle vs 15+ days)
  • Improved coverage/liability decision quality preventing expensive vehicle retrievals
  • Enhanced cost control on customer-selected shop repairs
  • 100% vehicle location visibility through daily task tracking

SOP 3: GYB Repair Process Management Primary Workflow ‚ñº
üéØ This is the bread and butter: 75% of claims follow this path. Smooth, fast, controlled repairs = happy customers + controlled costs.

Day 1: Vehicle Arrival at GYB

  • Confirm vehicle arrival in VICE claim notes
  • Verify GYB has customer contact info
  • Confirm estimate timeline (2-3 business days)
  • Authorize rental if applicable
  • Customer communication: "Your vehicle is at our partner shop, estimate in 2-3 days"

Days 2-4: GYB Estimate Creation & Submission

  • GYB completes teardown and damage assessment
  • Documents all damage with photos in CCC
  • Uploads estimate to CCC within 2-3 business days

Days 3-5: Estimate Review

CCC Auto-Approval or Manual Review:
  • CCC checks estimate against business rules automatically
  • If passes ‚Üí Auto-approved
  • If flagged ‚Üí Desk Review Appraiser performs manual review
<65% ACV (75% of estimates) REPAIRABLE ‚Üí Adjuster authorizes repair within 24 hours
65-75% ACV (15% of estimates) BORDERLINE ‚Üí Desk Review Appraiser makes final call in 24hrs
>70% ACV (10% of estimates) AUTOMATIC TL ‚Üí Do NOT authorize repair, create TL Task

Days 6-14: Repair Execution (Repairable Path)

  • Adjuster approves estimate in VICE within 24 hours
  • GYB orders parts and begins repair
  • Monitor for supplements (review within 24 hours)
  • Weekly progress check if repair >7 days
  • GYB completes final QA inspection
  • Customer picks up vehicle
  • Target: 14 days from FNOL to vehicle return
Supplement Management: If supplements push total to 65-70% ACV ‚Üí Create TL Task. If >70% ACV ‚Üí STOP WORK immediately, convert to TL.

Exception Handling - When Things Go Wrong

This section covers scenarios outside normal workflow. Use these procedures only when the situation matches the trigger conditions.
SOP 2A: Customer-Selected Shop - Enhanced Risk Management High Oversight ‚ñº
When This Applies: Customer selects a repair shop outside the GYB network.
Key Principle: MORE stringent oversight than GYB due to lack of contractual cost controls.

Required Actions (Summary)

  1. Document choice and manually create daily monitoring task in VICE
  2. Assign Desk Review Appraiser within 4 hours (mandatory, not optional)
  3. 100% manual review of estimate (no auto-approval)
  4. $250 supplement threshold (vs $500 for GYB) - all require Appraiser approval
  5. Daily progress monitoring - contact shop every 3 business days
  6. Quality verification before customer pickup
Zero Tolerance: No economic TL conversions after repair authorization. If estimate + supplements reach 65% ACV, create TL Task immediately.
Success Metrics:
  • 100% estimate review rate by Desk Review Appraiser
  • 100% supplement review for amounts >$250
  • Average repair cycle time <18 days (vs 14 for GYB)
SOP 2B: üö® Tow Yard Emergency - Elimination Protocol 24hr Removal ‚ñº
üö® ZERO TOLERANCE FOR TOW YARD DWELL TIME üö®
Vehicle MUST be removed within 24 hours - Focus on removal, not negotiation

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS (Same Day)

1. Unit Leader Escalation (Within 1 Hour)
  • Notify Unit Leader via task immediately
  • Document discovery time in claim notes
  • Obtain tow yard contact + daily storage rate
2. Emergency Damage Assessment (Within 2 Hours)
  • Obvious TL (rollover, burn, theft recovery) ‚Üí Route to Copart
  • Likely repairable (moderate damage, no structural) ‚Üí Route to GYB
  • Uncertain ‚Üí Default to GYB, transfer to Copart if TL confirmed
3. Emergency Tow Coordination (Within 4 Hours)
  • Contact Honk immediately
  • Request same-day or next-morning pickup
  • Notify tow yard of scheduled pickup to stop charges
  • Document tow authorization and tracking number
üí∞ Cost Handling: Tow yard cost negotiation will be handled by Total Loss Specialist as part of settlement process.
4. Root Cause Analysis (Within 48 Hours)
  • Unit Leader investigates how vehicle ended up at tow yard
  • Identify process gap or training need
  • Implement corrective action
SOP 2D: ‚ö† Storage Mitigation Process Coverage/Liability Issues ‚ñº
When This Applies: Projected TL vehicle that CANNOT go to Copart due to coverage or liability issues per Copart Assignment Matrix.

Objective: Minimize Storage Costs While Resolving Coverage/Liability

PHASE 1: Immediate Assessment (Day 0 - Within 4 Hours)
  1. Document coverage/liability issue in VICE
  2. Create "Storage Mitigation Active" task
  3. Verify current vehicle location
  4. If at tow yard ‚Üí Emergency removal within 24 hours (same as SOP 2B)
  5. Contact owner to explain situation and present options
PHASE 2: Storage Solution Selection (Day 0-1)
Option 1: Owner's Residence $0 - Preferred for all scenarios
Option 2: GYB Temporary Storage $0 (30 days free) - If owner has no space
Option 3: Selected Shop Storage $135-150/day - Requires Unit Leader approval
Never: Tow Yard $175+/day - Only if already there, remove immediately
PHASE 3: Resolution (Days 1-10)
  • Coverage investigation: 7-day resolution deadline
  • Liability investigation (3rd party): 5-day preliminary determination
  • Update owner every 3 days
  • Update "Storage Mitigation Active" task every 2 business days
PHASE 4: Final Disposition (Days 7-14)
  • Coverage/Liability CONFIRMED ‚Üí Transfer to TL Specialist, proceed to Copart
  • Coverage DENIED ‚Üí Return vehicle to owner, close claim
  • Partial Coverage ‚Üí Coordinate settlement with TL Specialist
Success Metrics:
‚Ä¢ 100% of vehicles out of tow yards within 24 hours
‚Ä¢ 90% at $0 storage within 48 hours
‚Ä¢ 80% resolved within 14 days
‚Ä¢ Average storage cost <$500 per claim

Total Loss Process - Early Task-Based Assessment

Philosophy: Identify and activate TL workflow as early as possible to minimize costs and expedite settlement. Total Loss Specialists begin parallel work immediately upon TL indicators.
SOP 4: Total Loss Process - 12-Day Settlement Target ‚ñº

PHASE 1: Early TL Task Activation (Days 0-2)

Trigger: Adjuster or Desk Review Appraiser manually creates "Early TL Assessment Task" when ANY TL indicator present:
  • Vehicle routed to Copart (obvious TL)
  • Damage suggests >70% severity
  • Estimate received showing ‚â•65% ACV
  • Multiple airbags + structural damage
  • Flood, fire, or theft recovery
Adjuster Action:
  • Create "Early TL Assessment" task in VICE within 4 hours
  • Assign to Total Loss Specialist queue
  • Include vehicle location, damage description, estimate if available
  • Continue normal claim processing while TL Specialist works in parallel
Total Loss Specialist - Immediate Parallel Work:
  • Run preliminary ACV valuation in CCC
  • Research comparable vehicles
  • Pull title information, identify lienholder
  • Contact lienholder for payoff quote if financed
  • Prepare settlement calculation template
  • Set up salvage auction notification with Copart

PHASE 2: Final TL Determination (Days 1-3)

Desk Review Appraiser:
  • Reviews estimate or damage assessment
  • Compares repair cost to ACV
  • If ‚â•70% ACV ‚Üí Confirms total loss
  • If 65-70% ACV ‚Üí Makes determination within 24 hours
  • Manually updates task to "TL Confirmed" status
Total Loss Specialist:
  • Receives "TL Confirmed" task notification
  • Reviews all parallel work already completed
  • Confirms vehicle location (Copart or GYB)
  • Finalizes settlement offer
  • Target: Settlement offer ready within 24 hours of TL confirmation

PHASE 3: Settlement Offer (Days 2-5)

  • Call owner to explain TL determination
  • Present settlement breakdown (ACV, salvage, deductible)
  • Email formal settlement letter with documentation
  • TL Specialist manually creates task: "Awaiting Owner Documents"
Required Documents: Title, signed settlement agreement, proof of loss, license plate return

PHASE 4: Payment Processing (Days 6-12)

  • Verify all documents received and complete
  • Process payment within 3 business days
  • If financed: Two-party check or direct payoff to lienholder
  • If owned: Check directly to owner
  • TL Specialist creates task: "Payment Issued - Awaiting Title Transfer"

Tow Yard Cost Negotiation (If Applicable)

Total Loss Specialist Responsibility: If vehicle incurred tow yard charges before being moved:
  • Request itemized invoice for all charges
  • Challenge excessive rates (>$175/day)
  • Negotiate to pay only actual days stored
  • Escalate to Unit Leader via task if unreasonable
  • Include tow yard charges in final settlement calculation

PHASE 5: Salvage & Closure

  • Vehicle enters Copart auction after title received
  • Auction completes within 1-2 weeks
  • Salvage proceeds credited within 48 hours
  • TL Specialist closes claim in VICE
‚úì TARGET CYCLE TIME: 12 DAYS
Days 0-1: TL indicators ‚Üí Early Task created ‚Üí Parallel work begins
Days 1-2: Final TL determination, vehicle at Copart/GYB
Days 2-3: Settlement offer presented
Days 4-8: Owner document collection
Days 9-12: Payment and title transfer

Success Metric: 80% of TL claims settled within 12 days
